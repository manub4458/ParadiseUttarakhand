Welcome to Our Store

Where Your Adventure
Begins

Paradise Uttarakhand is a premier travel company based in the scenic state of Uttarakhand, India. Founded by Gaurav Major, our company specializes in providing a wide range of travel services, including char dham yatra packages and taxi services in Uttarakhand.

Our company was established with a vision to offer exceptional travel experiences to our clients. We believe that travel should be an immersive and transformative experience that allows you to explore new destinations, learn about different cultures, and create memories that last a lifetime. With this in mind, we have curated a range of travel packages that cater to different interests and preferences.

Our char dham yatra packages are especially popular among pilgrims who wish to visit the holy shrines of Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath, and Badrinath. These shrines are considered to be the most sacred in Hinduism and attract thousands of devotees every year. We offer comprehensive packages that include transportation, accommodation, and sightseeing, ensuring that your pilgrimage is smooth and hassle-free.

In addition to our char dham yatra packages, we also offer a range of other travel services, including taxi services in Uttarakhand. Our fleet of well-maintained vehicles is equipped with modern amenities and driven by experienced drivers who prioritize safety and comfort. Whether you need a taxi for a short trip or a long journey, we can provide you with the perfect vehicle to suit your needs.

At Paradise Uttarakhand, we pride ourselves on our commitment to customer satisfaction. We believe that every traveler is unique, and we work closely with our clients to understand their preferences and create personalized itineraries. Our team of experts is available round the clock to answer your queries and provide assistance whenever needed. We also place a strong emphasis on sustainability and responsible tourism. We believe that travel should not only be enjoyable but also ethical and sustainable. As such, we strive to minimize our environmental impact and promote responsible tourism practices.

In conclusion, Paradise Uttarakhand is your go-to travel partner for all your travel needs in Uttarakhand. With our comprehensive travel packages, personalized services, and commitment to sustainability, we ensure that you have a memorable and fulfilling travel experience. Get in touch with us today to start planning your next adventure in paradise.
